Itemslide.JS
Init | Options | Methods

  • Download Now
  • Tested on:
    iPad Mini
    Nexus 4
    iPhone 4
  • Very light roughly 5kb
  • To use simply write:
    $("ul").initslide();

  • Sliding is Cool!
  • Enjoy!
  • Options:
    duration - duration of swipe animation (default: 275)
    pan_sensitivity - panning sensitivity (default: 10)
    swipe_sensitivity - swiping sensitivity (default: 250)
  • Methods:
    next() - goes to next slide
    previous() - goes to previous slide
    gotoSlide(i) - goes to a specific slide by index
